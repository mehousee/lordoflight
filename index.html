Inclusive Christian Community (For real).

Lord of Light Lutheran Church & Campus Ministry is an inclusive, intergenerational campus congregation serving the University of Michigan and the surrounding community. We are part of the LuMin Network in the ELCA, as well as the interdenominational Zoe Progressive Christian Network. Deeply rooted in a tradition of social justice, we are committed to showing God's love to all people.
We are openly and intentionally welcoming and affirming of everyone as beloved creations of God: Of any gender identity or expression, sexual orientation, ethnicity, denomination, or faith. We explicitly reject systems of white supremacy, and we are committed to working for racial justice and equality.
